I am an associate professor in computer science at the Computer Science Department of Karlstad University in Sweden. My expertise is in teaching Operating Systems, Computer Networking, and IoT. My research interests include low latency networking, web performance, mobile broadband networks, and IoT. I have numerous publications in top conferences and journals and have been a key member in various European Union projects such as RITE, MONROE, NEAT, and 5GENESIS. Currently, I lead a subproject in the DRIVE project.
